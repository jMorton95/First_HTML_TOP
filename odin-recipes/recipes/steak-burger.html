An American style Beef Cheese Burger!

This burger was hand crafted by an exquisite chef in a busy New York Steak House!

Ingredient List

  • Half-Pound Steak Burger Patty
  • Pickles, Tomato, Lettuce, Onion
  • Burger Cheese
  • Burger Sauce
  • Seeded Brioche Bun

Recipe!

  1. Prepare the vegetables.
  2. Oil a pan, turn the heat up high, add the Burger Patty and sear each side.
  3. Slice the Brioche Bun, spread Burger Sauce on each side of the bun.
  4. Add the lettuce and onions on the bottom of the Brioche Bun.
  5. Once cooked through, add the Burger Patty ontop of the bun, top with the Burger Cheese.
  6. Add the Lettuce and Pickles ontop of the bun, then finish by adding the top of the Bun!
