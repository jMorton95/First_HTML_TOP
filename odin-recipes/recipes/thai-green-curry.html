Thai Green Curry Recipe!

This is an example of a Thai Green Curry made by a professional chef!

Ingredients List:

  • Chicken Breast
  • Coconut Milk
  • Thai Green Chilli Paste
  • Garlic, Green Beans, Onion
  • Salt, Spices, Palm Sugar
  • Olive Oil
  • Rice

Recipe!

  1. Begin cooking the Rice in a Rice Cooker or a Pan.
  2. Heat a pan or dutch oven to medium heat and add Olive Oil.
  3. Once heated, add the Coconut Milk and Chilli Paste. Stir until dissolved and thick.
  4. Dice the Chicken Breast and add to the pan.
  5. Slice the onions to your preferred size, add to the pan.
  6. Chop and crush the Garlic, add with the Salt and other Spices to the pan.
  7. Add the Green Beans and Palm sugar.
  8. Once the Chicken has cooked through, serve with rice!
