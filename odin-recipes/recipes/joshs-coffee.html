A recipe for Klavelon's favourite coffee

A coffee brewed by an excellent barista.

Ingredients list!
  • Ground Coffee
  • Sugar
  • Milk
  • Coffee Mug
  • Kettle
Recipe!
  1. Boil hot water in a kettle
  2. Add a heaped teaspoon of Ground Coffee to the mug.
  3. Add 2 heaped spoonfuls of Sugar to the mug.
  4. Fill the mug up two third of the way with the boiled water and stir well.
  5. Top up the mug with Milk and stir again.
